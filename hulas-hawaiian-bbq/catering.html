Catering

Details

Full Catering Price List Parties for 50-500 people. Prices below includes white rice, your meat or seafood choices, 2 salads, 1 dessert item.

  • 1 meat $15.99 per person
  • 2 meat $19.99 per person
  • 3 meat $25.99 per person

Our typical Menu usually consists of 2 or 3 meat dishes, white rice and one choice of a salad. Prices usually run anywhere from $15.99 per person up to $34.99 per person depending on whether it is a "drop off" type of service or "full service catering." Prices will also vary depending on the type of meats and seafoods selected and whether you choose to have "grill on site" service. We have 2 large grills that can accommodate up to 300 people. If you are on a tight budget, you may choose from our "ala carte" menu. We can prepare any item and deliver it to the site of your party. Please call for "ala carte" pricing. All items are served in "large" food pans which will feed up to 30 people. We work with each customer individually based on their projected budget.
